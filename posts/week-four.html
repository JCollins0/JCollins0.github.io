What was your experience of Project #1?

The complexity of Project #1 was overall not that bad. The problem was pretty easy to solve especially in python. I know that the workflow was a little overkill for Collatz, but it will definitely be useful for the group project later on, as there will be way more stuff to do.

What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

My pick of the week is TexStudio. TexStudio is a LaTeX editor that has auto complete, an integrated PDF viewer, spell checking, word frequency checking, inline formula previewing, syntax highlighting, and much much more. I find TexStudio to be better than most editors that I have used before, especially online editors. The autocomplete prety much puts it over the top for me.
